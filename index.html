POWERFUL BLOCKCHAIN TECHNOLOGIES

Multi-chain

Multi-chain can turn the current complex, fragmented, and separated blockchains into a collection of chains which is capable of operating seamlessly, without interruption. It solves the scaling problem by reaching new users in different ecosystems. It also takes advantage of each chain such as low transaction fees, fast speed to overcome the main chain's shortcomings such as congestion, low throughput, etc. Moreover, it minimizes the risk to the project itself because when a project deploys on many different blockchains, if there is a problem with a certain chain, the project will not be affected too much.

Web3 authentication

Web3 authentication is a login tool which can be integrated with any wallet, platform, chain and social login. Web3 websites and applications run on or at least provide for users to interact with specific blockchains. Therefore, there must be a secure way for users to connect to those particular cryptocurrency networks. Using Web3Auth, users can connect to a selected network. Once authenticated, they will be given the right to interact with other authenticated users on that network. As such, every Web3 dApp needs this type of authentication.

CEX

Due to the high trading volume, CEX has high liquidity. The exchanges can meet payment obligations quickly and easily. The bustling activity on the centralized trading venues ensures many buy and sell orders to guarantee market-friendly trading. CEX is based on an individual and central infrastructure. This enables fast transaction exchange almost in real-time. The algorithms of the leading exchanges can process thousands of orders per second. Traders can react to changing market situations without waiting. The major mainstream exchanges offer a wide range of digital currencies with countless trading pairs. At the same time, deposits and withdrawals in fiat currency are also possible.

Decentralized

Instead of passing through one central computer or verification control, the validation and verification of a transaction is done by the blockchain network.
